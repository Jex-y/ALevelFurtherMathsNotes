\section*{Operations}
Suppose we have two functions:\\ 
$f: A \rightarrow B \qquad g: B \rightarrow C $\\
We can compose them using the $ \circ $ operator\\
\subsection*{Example 1:}
\begin{align*}
g \circ f: & A \rightarrow C \\
